Current Research Focus

1. Formal Theory of Topological Orders

My work involves classifying fractionalized phases of matter in (2+1)D and beyond. I utilize the framework of unitary modular tensor categories (UMTCs) to describe the algebraic structure of anyon fusion and braiding.

2. Topological Quantum Computation

Investigating fault-tolerant logical gates through the lens of lattice models. I am particularly interested in the algebraic properties of the Toric Code and broader quantum double models $D(G)$.

$$ A_v = \prod_{j \in \text{star}(v)} \sigma^x_j, \quad B_p = \prod_{j \in \text{boundary}(p)} \sigma^z_j $$

3. Categorical Quantum Mechanics

Applying string diagrammatic calculus (graphical calculus) to simplify and formalize calculations in many-body physics and TQFT.
